Best Rooftop Bars in Lima — Views, Cocktails & Sunset Spots

Lima's clifftop setting overlooking the Pacific makes it perfect for rooftop bars. From swanky Miraflores lounges to artsy Barranco terraces, here are the best spots to sip cocktails with spectacular views.

2026-03-10 • 8 min read

Rooftop Bars in Miraflores

Miraflores has the highest concentration of rooftop bars in Lima. The JW Marriott's rooftop lounge offers panoramic ocean views. Several bars along Calle Manuel Bonilla and the Malecón area have terraces with sunset views. Expect craft pisco sours, ceviche bites, and ambient music. Prices are premium — cocktails run S/35-55 — but the views are worth it. Many spots have happy hours between 5-7pm.

Rooftop Bars in Barranco

Barranco's rooftop scene is more bohemian. Bars here tend to be artier, with live music, local craft beers, and a younger crowd. The views aren't always ocean-facing, but the atmosphere makes up for it. Several new rooftop bars have opened along the Bajada de Baños area. Expect more affordable prices than Miraflores — cocktails around S/25-40.

Best Sunset Spots

For the ultimate sunset experience, head to a bar facing west along the Malecón before 5:30pm. Lima sunsets are legendary — especially between May and September when the sky turns orange and pink over the Pacific. Reserve a table at least an hour before sunset during weekends. Some bars have 'sunset menus' with paired drinks and snacks.

What to Order

Pisco sour is the obvious choice — and it's genuinely excellent at most rooftop bars. Chilcanos (pisco + ginger ale + lime) are refreshing. Many bars now feature creative cocktails using Peruvian ingredients like lucuma, maracuyá, and chicha morada. Wine lovers should try Peruvian Tannat. Craft beer options have exploded in recent years.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best rooftop bar in Lima?

It depends on your vibe. For ocean views, try rooftop bars along the Malecón in Miraflores. For atmosphere, Barranco terraces are unbeatable. For luxury, hotel rooftops are the way to go.

Do rooftop bars in Lima have dress codes?

Most are smart-casual. Hotel rooftops may be stricter — avoid flip-flops and beachwear. Barranco bars are more relaxed. When in doubt, clean jeans and a nice shirt work everywhere.

When is the best time for sunset drinks in Lima?

Sunset is around 6pm year-round. Arrive by 5pm to secure a good table, especially on weekends. May-September has the most dramatic sunsets.
