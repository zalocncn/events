Lima Craft Beer Scene — Best Breweries, Bars & Taprooms

Lima's craft beer revolution is in full swing. Local breweries are producing world-class beers, taprooms are popping up across the city, and beer festivals draw huge crowds. Here's your guide to the best craft beer in Lima.

2026-04-10 • 7 min read

Top Breweries & Taprooms

Barranco Beer Company is one of Lima's pioneers — great taproom with food. Barbarian is widely available and consistently excellent. Zenith, Candelaria, and Sierra Andina offer unique Peruvian-inspired brews. Many breweries have taprooms where you can taste directly. Miraflores and Barranco have the highest concentration of craft beer venues.

Best Craft Beer Bars

Beyond brewery taprooms, Lima has dedicated craft beer bars stocking dozens of local and international brews. These spots often have rotating taps, beer flights for tasting, and knowledgeable staff. Look for bars in Miraflores, Barranco, and increasingly in San Isidro. Prices: S/15-25 per pint for craft. Happy hours exist at many spots.

Peruvian Beer Styles to Try

Peruvian brewers are experimenting with local ingredients. Look for beers made with quinoa, purple corn (chicha-inspired), lucuma, maracuyá (passion fruit), coca leaf, and Peruvian peppers. These unique flavors make Lima's craft beer scene stand out globally. IPAs and pale ales are popular, but stouts and sours are growing fast.

Beer Events & Festivals

Lima hosts several beer festivals throughout the year. Craft beer fairs in parks, brewery collaborations, and tasting events are common. Check Lime for upcoming beer events. Many bars have weekly events like trivia night, live music + beer, or brewer meet-and-greets.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does craft beer cost in Lima?

Expect S/15-25 per pint at bars and taprooms. Beer flights (4-5 tasters) are S/25-40. Bottles from breweries are S/10-20. It's more expensive than mass-market beer but worth it.

What's the best craft brewery in Lima?

Barranco Beer Company, Barbarian, and Zenith are top picks. Each has a different specialty. Visit taprooms to find your favorite.
