Best Date Night Spots in Lima — Romantic Restaurants, Bars & Activities

Lima is one of South America's most romantic cities — dramatic ocean sunsets, world-class dining, intimate cocktail bars, and a warmth in the air that makes everything feel a little more special. Whether you're planning a first date or celebrating an anniversary, here are the best date night spots in Lima for 2026.

2026-03-08 • 9 min read

Romantic Restaurants for Every Budget

Lima's restaurant scene is legendary, and many of its best spots are inherently romantic. For a splurge, Maido (ranked among the world's best) serves Nikkei cuisine in an intimate setting — expect to spend S/250-400 per person. Central, Virgilio Martínez's famous restaurant, offers a multi-course journey through Peru's ecosystems that's as much an experience as a meal.

For more accessible romance, Cala in Barranco sits right on the ocean with candlelit tables overlooking the Pacific — stunning at sunset (S/80-150 per person). Isolina, also in Barranco, serves traditional Peruvian comfort food in a beautifully designed space (S/50-80). And for a casual but charming first date, El Mercado in Miraflores offers Rafael Osterling's ceviche in a relaxed, market-inspired setting (S/60-100).

Cocktail Bars & Sunset Spots

After dinner (or instead of it), Lima's cocktail bars provide the perfect setting for intimate conversation. Ayahuasca in Barranco is the most stunning venue — a restored mansion where every room feels like a different world. Order craft pisco cocktails and explore the multiple floors and terraces.

For sunset views, the Malecón in Miraflores can't be beaten — walk along the clifftop and stop at one of the park benches as the sky turns orange and pink. If you want a more polished sunset experience, the terrace at Cala or the rooftop at Hotel B in Barranco offer drinks with panoramic ocean views.

  • ✓Ayahuasca (Barranco) — Stunning mansion, craft cocktails, S/25-40
  • ✓Hotel B terrace (Barranco) — Rooftop sunset views, sophisticated vibe
  • ✓Malecón sunset walk (Miraflores) — Free, beautiful, endlessly romantic
  • ✓Huaringas Bar (Miraflores) — Pisco cocktails, cozy atmosphere, S/20-35

Unique Date Activities Beyond Dinner

Lima offers plenty of memorable date activities that go beyond the typical dinner-and-drinks formula. Take a sunset paragliding tandem flight over the Miraflores cliffs (S/200-350 per person) for an adrenaline-fueled bonding experience. Or book a couples cooking class where you learn to make ceviche and pisco sours together (S/150-250 per person).

For something more low-key, catch a live jazz or bossa nova show at Jazz Zone or La Noche de Barranco. The Circuito Mágico del Agua (Magic Water Circuit) in the Parque de la Reserva features elaborate fountain shows with lights and music — cheesy but undeniably romantic, especially on a warm evening.

Art lovers can visit the MATE museum (Mario Testino's photography gallery) followed by a walk through Barranco's street art scene. And for a truly unique experience, the Catacombs of San Francisco in Centro Histórico offer a hauntingly beautiful underground tour.

First Date Ideas in Lima

First dates require a balance of interesting-enough-to-impress and casual-enough-to-be-comfortable. A walk along the Malecón from Larcomar to Barranco followed by drinks at a chill bar is the classic Lima first date — it gives you time to talk, beautiful scenery, and a natural endpoint.

The weekend artisan markets at Parque Kennedy or Reducto provide a fun, low-pressure browsing activity with plenty of snack vendors. Or try a food tour through Surquillo market — guided tours (S/80-150) include ceviche, exotic fruit, and chicha tastings in a colorful market setting.

Keep it simple and authentic: Lima's charm is in its warmth, its ocean views, and its incredible food. You don't need an expensive restaurant to have a great date — some of the most romantic moments happen over S/15 ceviches at a hole-in-the-wall with plastic chairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most romantic restaurant in Lima?

Cala in Barranco, with its oceanfront terrace and candlelit tables at sunset, is arguably the most romantic setting. For food quality, Maido and Central are world-class experiences. For traditional charm, Isolina and Huaca Pucllana (dining beside illuminated ruins) are unforgettable.

What are good first date ideas in Lima?

A Malecón sunset walk followed by drinks in Barranco is the classic Lima first date. Other options: food market tour in Surquillo, artisan market browsing at Parque Kennedy, or ceviche at a casual spot like El Mercado or La Mar.

How much should I budget for a date night in Lima?

A nice date night costs S/150-300 total for two (dinner + drinks). A budget date can work for S/60-100 (casual restaurant + walk). A splurge experience (fine dining + cocktail bar) runs S/400-800+ for two.

Are there any free romantic activities in Lima?

Yes! The Malecón sunset walk, Barranco street art tour, Puente de los Suspiros stroll, and the Circuito Mágico del Agua (S/4 entrance) are all beautiful, romantic activities that cost little to nothing.

Conclusion

Keep it simple and authentic: Lima's charm is in its warmth, its ocean views, and its incredible food. You don't need an expensive restaurant to have a great date — some of the most romantic moments happen over S/15 ceviches at a hole-in-the-wall with plastic chairs.
