Frequently Asked Questions

How much does karaoke cost in Lima?

Open-mic bars are usually free (just buy drinks). Private rooms cost S/50-100 per hour. Drinks range from S/15-30 for cocktails. Budget S/50-100 per person for a full night out.

Do karaoke bars in Lima have English songs?

Yes, most karaoke venues in Miraflores and tourist areas have extensive English catalogs. Japanese-style rooms often have multilingual options. Smaller local bars may be Spanish-only.

What's the best night for karaoke in Lima?

Friday and Saturday are busiest and most fun. Thursday is good for shorter waits. Some bars have special karaoke nights midweek with drink specials.
