Weekend Food Markets

Every weekend, neighborhoods across Lima host food markets and fairs. Barranco's artisan markets feature local producers, craft beer, and street food vendors. Miraflores has weekend brunch markets near the parks. Surco hosts family-friendly food fairs. These are free to enter and perfect for Saturday or Sunday mornings.

Cooking Classes & Food Experiences

For a hands-on experience, Lima's cooking classes are unforgettable. Learn to make ceviche from a local chef, take a pisco sour masterclass, or join a market tour followed by a cooking session. Many of these are listed on Eventbrite and appear in Lime's calendar. Prices range from $30-100 per person.

Restaurant Weeks & Special Events

Lima's restaurant weeks offer prix fixe menus at top restaurants for accessible prices. Several happen throughout the year, usually in March, June, and September. It's the best way to try restaurants that normally have month-long waitlists. Check Lime for dates and participating restaurants.
